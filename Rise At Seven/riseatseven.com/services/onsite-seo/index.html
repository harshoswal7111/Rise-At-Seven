Our unique approach

How we tackle SEO for our clients

At Rise at Seven, our approach to technical SEO isn’t about ticking boxes - it’s about moving the needle in ways that genuinely impact your bottom line.

First, we keep it commercial. That means we zero in on your KPIs - not just traffic for traffic’s sake, or ranking, but the right kind of visibility for the products or services that matter most to your business. We align SEO strategy with your core goals from day one.

Second, we believe in testing, not guessing. We know SEO changes can take time, resources, and sign-off, so rather than rolling out sitewide changes blindly, we run controlled tests, proven by data, before we scale.

Finally, we prioritise ruthlessly. You won’t get a long-winded 100-point checklist from us. You’ll get the “6” things that’ll make an immediate impact, “34” that’ll help in the medium term, and “60” best-practice items to tackle when you can (or we do it for you). It’s a focused, no-waste approach that delivers faster wins and long-term performance.

This is SEO designed for performance, clarity, and commercial growth.

FAQ's

How many people are in the SEO team?
How many people are in the SEO team?

We have a 75-strong team globally, all trained in search first SEO, content, Digital PR or social. Our onsite SEO, technical team, data reporting, SEO strategy and link building team spans across 60% of the overall headcount.

How are we optimising for LLMs/AI search?
How are we optimising for LLMs/AI search?

We combine the best technical, content experience and off-site brand and authority signals to show up in AI search and large language models. By building semantic relevancy signals, we’re engineering AI to help humans associate our clients' brands with the categories they want to lead in. See AI/LLM SEO case study examples here: AI Overview, LLMs

How much does SEO cost?
How much does SEO cost?

Our SEO costs range from £4K a month up to £150K a month, depending on site traffic, territories/markets, and services. For those looking for a strategic consultancy and briefing, expect to pay anywhere between £4-6K a month. If you want the full suite of SEO services, including content and digital PR (links), the starting fee is £7K upwards. Our clients paying £50K a month are large-scale sites for multiple services within organic marketing (SEO, Content and Digital PR) across multiple markets and multiple channels.

What key metrics do you report on for organic growth?
What key metrics do you report on for organic growth?

We start all our SEO relationships identifying the north star, setting an organic growth target, whether it's revenue, traffic, share of search, branded search, share of SERP or other. See our SEO case studies on how we report on success for a number of clients.

What industries are your SEO team experienced in?
What industries are your SEO team experienced in?

Our SEO team has worked in the industry for 25+ years and has experience in nearly every industry. We have case studies in E-comm/retail, fashion, travel, finance, banking, health, and regulated industries, tech, gaming, sports, FMCG, home and more.

How do you work with clients with an existing SEO team for execution?
How do you work with clients with an existing SEO team for execution?

We work with clients in many different ways. If a client has a team already existing in-house we can support on the basics up to the advanced, we can give strategic consultancy and briefs with in-depth roadmaps, or we can help all the way to execution. Many of our clients like us to provide training with their teams, and hand-hold through certain tasks. We're always up for sitting in your office to support, up skill or move things faster when the business needs.
