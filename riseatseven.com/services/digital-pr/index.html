Our unique approach

How we run Digital PR for our clients

At Rise at Seven, we have a multi-strategy approach to using PR to move consumers down the funnel and own their category.

PUSH - You nearly always have a brand or product message you want to push to the world through PR and Media. Our push strategies include product PR, press office, data reports and studies, thought leadership and commentary. This is often a monthly tactic to push consistent messages, driving a dominant share of voice within your category.

PULL - Alongside this, we have an always-on pull strategy, pulling you into media moments that are happening. We track the media so you don't have to, reacting with products, commentary, data or fun creatives to stay relevant and IN culture. Speed is the key here.

POW - And finally, pow moments. You can’t lead a category without creating PR work people remember. We run bimonthly, quarterly or biannually PR campaigns, often tied with social and creators to drive brand demand at scale.

All of these together drive category leaders, and we have case studies coming out of our ears to prove it.

FAQ's

How many people are in the Digital PR team?
How many people are in the Digital PR team?

We have a 30-strong Digital PR team globally, all trained in traditional PR, digital PR, product PR, data studies and creative campaigns. 10 of our team are over in the States.

How do you work with traditional PR teams?
How do you work with traditional PR teams?

Working with traditional PR teams is what we do best, where others don’t. We work with their roadmaps to understand what we can do to amplify their product and brand messaging through our push, pull, pow strategies. We are constantly finding ways to integrate top of the funnel activity with search. We often become the agency that supports traditional teams with ideas to create an integrated approach.

What kind of results should be expected from Digital PR?
What kind of results should be expected from Digital PR?

It all really depends on what you are spending, but ultimately if PR isn't driving organic traffic visibility, branded search and or lower cost of acquiring a customer it isn't working.

How much does Digital PR cost?
How much does Digital PR cost?

Our digital PR services range from £5K/$6.8K a month up to £70K+/$100K+ a month, depending on your needs. We have 3 ways of delivering Digital PR broken down into Push, Pull, Pow. Most of our clients run these in unison in order to become a category leader.

What key metrics do you report on for Digital PR?
What key metrics do you report on for Digital PR?

We have a range of metrics from the number of press coverage and links, traffic from media, visibility growth, share of voice, brand awareness/search, follow vs no follow, and our own relevancy score to ensure all stories are as closely relevant to our product or category as possible.

What do you do if Digital PR isn't driving organic growth/traffic?
What do you do if Digital PR isn't driving organic growth/traffic?

We don't sell you digital PR without a search strategy - if things aren't driving growth, it's usually because the website isn't technically sound or converting as we like. We offer technical EO, content and offsite social search services alongside digital PR to be fully responsible for driving organic growth.

How fast can we see the impact of Digital PR and get results/coverage?
How fast can we see the impact of Digital PR and get results/coverage?

It all depends how fast you can move. Our PR team tries to show you results within week 1 if you've bought one of our pull tactics - Rise Live. This is the fastest way to see results. If you couple that with campaigns and data reports you are on your way to having activity always on.

What if we can't be super fast or work in a regulated industry?
What if we can't be super fast or work in a regulated industry?

Don't worry, we have lots of experience here with a variety of products and services so we work at a pace and process that serves you well. From our work with Capital One, one of the biggest regulated financial brands in the world, to Axa Health - we have experience working with many brands and can show you how to still achieve the same results.

How long does it take to run a digital PR campaign?
How long does it take to run a digital PR campaign?

A typical campaign shouldn't take longer than 4-6 weeks from start to end. We also have fast campaign processes which are less than 2 weeks if there's a moment we want to go faster for.

Do you do Digital PR training for inhouse brands?
Do you do Digital PR training for inhouse brands?

All the time. We have trained brands ranging from Lush Cosmetics, to large-scale gambling brands, Hendricks Gin, Wayfair and more. Get in touch to enquire about a training day with us.
