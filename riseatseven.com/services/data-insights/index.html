The data behind category leaders

Category leadership measurement

A true category leader isn’t just the brand with the loudest voice- it’s the one that consistently wins at both demand and discovery through a robust metrics-driven framework.

Using our Demand & Discovery proposition, we measure leadership across the five key stages in Googles messy middle - Triggers (demand), Explore (discovery), Evaluate (discovery) Purchase (demand & discovery), Experience (demand & discovery) - with a blend of quantitative and qualitative indicators.

Multi Channel Search

Creating Demand and Converting Customers

On the demand side, we track things like:

  • Brand + category search volumes
  • Share of Search (percentage of category searches your brand captures),
  • Click-Through Rate within SERP features,
  • Conversion Rate from organic traffic.

On the discovery side, we monitor:
  • Rankings
  • Visibility
  • Social Reach (total unique impressions across social channels)
  • Engagement Velocity (rate at which new audiences engage with brand content)
  • Share of search and share of media

Summary

We layer on multi-channel attribution to ensure we’re not only sparking interest but also steering high-intent audiences toward purchase. By benchmarking these metrics against competitors - and tying them back to business outcomes like new customer acquisition cost and lifetime value - we can definitively say who truly leads the category.
